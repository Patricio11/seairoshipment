Documents for Shipments – We can additional as we get different products 

Meat Reefers and Dairy Reefers
-	Commercial Invoice 
-	Packing List 
-	Copy of import permit
-	Bill of lading 
-	Export Certificate 
-	PPECB Health Certificate 
-	Halaal Certificate 
-	SAD500 customs entry 
-	Certificate of Origin 

Fish Reefers 
-	Commercial Invoice 
-	Packing List 
-	Copy of import permit
-	Bill of lading 
-	PPECB Export Certificate 
-	NRCS Health Certificate 
-	SAD500 customs entry 
-	Certificate of Origin 

Fruit exports 
-	Commercial Invoice 
-	Packing List 
-	Copy of import permit
-	Bill of lading 
-	PPECB Export Certificate 
-	Phyto Sanitary certificate 
-	SAD500 customs entry 
-	Certificate of Origin 
-	CoA
-	SGS - Applicable to certain countries 


Other products with different documents needed
-	Wine (wine needs SAWIS certificates) 
-	Hunting Trophies (trophies needs CITIES certificates) 
-	Dry food good and canned goods (they don’t need certain temperature sensitive documents such as PPECB)
